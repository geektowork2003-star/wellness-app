User Guide
🕐 Today Tab
Your daily dashboard. Log steps (type + Save), water (slider + Save Water), and tick off habits.
Close My Day at night to get your AI day review.
🍎 Food Tab
1. Select meal type → describe food → tap + Add Meal (saves instantly)
2. Add meals throughout the day
3. Tap Analyse & Save My Day for AI calorie breakdown
Limit: 2 analyses per day, resets at midnight.
📈 Progress Tab
Log weight daily. View weight trend and 14-day steps charts. Generate a weekly AI report anytime.
📋 Plan Tab
Your personalised AI wellness plan. Try it for a week before regenerating — tap Edit My Plan to add your own notes anytime.
⚙ More Tab
Profile — update name, weights, calorie target.
Health Parameters — enter lab values for smarter AI advice.
Feedback — tell us what you think.
🌙 Close My Day
Tap every evening. Select what worked + tomorrow's challenge, then tap Confirm & Close Day for your full AI day review (food, habits, water, activity, goals). Day resets at midnight.
💡 Quick Tips
✓ Add meals as you eat — don't wait till evening
✓ Enter health lab values in More for better AI advice
✓ Close My Day every night — best daily coaching habit
✓ Each family member needs their own account
